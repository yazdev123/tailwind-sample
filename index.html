about tailwind

Who we are and what we do

Tailwind IT Solutions is based in the Judean Hills of Israel, located about 20 minutes from Jerusalem. The Hebrew name for "Tailwind" is "Ruach Gabit" (רוח גבית). "Ruach" (רוח) means "wind" or "sprint" and "gabit" (גבית) means "towards the direction of our back."

Tailwind IT Solutions was founded in 2019 by two experienced web developers: Joseph Magen and Tomi Frank.

We believe that web technology should serve as a "tailwind" pushing your business forward. Unfortunately, for many businesses, it can be a "headwind" slowing you down. Tailwind is a custom web development boutique that specializes in creating custom-made tailored solutions for your business that truly serve as a "tailwind" (ruach gabit) to propel you forward.
